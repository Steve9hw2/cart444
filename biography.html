Steve Berthiaume was born in Longueuil, receiving a BFA in 2024 from Concordia University and a DEC from Champlain Regional College in 2020. He is a game designer with experience in 3D modelling, programming and art with works varying from digital games on various platforms to tangible and 3D art. His work includes an investigation of the implementation of artificial intelligence with traditional visual novel game formats, performed in 2023. In 2024, he created a game centered on his experiences in retail, collaborating with various coworkers to create a realistic and thorough parody of the life of retail workers. His time in retail lasted from 2018 to 2024, with a promotion to the role of second assistant manager occurring in 2021.
