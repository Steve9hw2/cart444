My works consist of games spread across various platforms such as web games, executables, or tangible media such as cards which explore various themes of identity while also treading new paths in exploring compelling avenues of game design. My works vary greatly in their form and feel but retain my underlying pursuit of exploration and individuality. In all of my work lies a statement on the power of understanding, creativity, and idiosyncrasy.
I seek to explore why games are designed the way they are, stylizing my exploration as an experience for the audience to undergo with me. I pursue works which captivate me in their craft, expressing such passion in layers of variation. My works vary from comedic to somber tones, while maintaining a level of creative subversion and depth, providing unique moments of responsivity that elevate the individualized experience.
